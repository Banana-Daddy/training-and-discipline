TStop 02 · Who we are

Personal
training,
properly.

We are not a gym. We are a small, disciplined room — four trainers, one disco ball, glass garage doors, the mountains a hundred yards away — built for the work of one person at a time.

The studio sits at the corner of training and discipline — coincident with care, stillness, and the long pathway of recovery. You don’t come here to be one of many. You come because someone is watching, programming, correcting, and lifting alongside you.

We welcome locals, seasonal residents, travelers, beginners, and elite movers. We’re proudly inclusive. We work with your physical therapist, chiropractor, or surgeon when you need us to. We’ll meet you where you are.

“Watching, correcting, programming.”
The work is in the room.
