The studio

A small studio
built deliberately
unlike a gym.

We developed Training & Discipline for the client who desires an upscale training experience in a boutique space — away from the distractions and hustle of large gyms. Charcoal walls, a disco ball over the squat rack, glass garage doors that open onto Tahquitz Canyon Way, a bamboo-floored yoga room.

Every workout is safe, challenging, and fun — every time. Exercise "buddies" are not certified personal trainers. There — we said it. Our team has decades of experience across corporate fitness, academic and sports coaching, and healing modalities.

Programs & services

What you
can train for.

Every plan is custom-built for your wellness needs and documented at every visit, so progress is clear at a glance. We work with bodies new to training and bodies recovering from injury alike.

  1. 01
    T Strength

    Strength training, beginner to elite.

    Programmed by a CSCS and an ACE-CES. From a first deadlift to your strongest year. Plans are written down so you (and your trainer) can see what's working.
  2. 02
    D Body composition

    Weight loss — that lasts.

    A sustainable plan written for your life — not a 30-day reset. We build it around what you actually like doing, with realistic milestones.
  3. 03
    C Mobility

    Stretching & flexibility.

    A dedicated 30-, 45-, or 60-minute session — not a 60-second warm-up. Useful for the desk-bound, the runners, and the recently-90.
  4. 04
    S Yoga

    Individualized yoga instruction.

    1-on-1 with a 500-hr E-RYT and Yoga Therapy specialist. In our bamboo-floored room with the Buddha screen — not a Zoom box.
  5. 05
    P Medical-condition support

    Programs that work with your doctor.

    Completed your cardiac rehab, chiropractic care, physical therapy, recent joint replacement with your doctor or therapist? Have a neurological condition that needs support? We can develop exercise programs that may help you manage your condition and improve your well-being.
Meet the team

Four trainers.
Decades of training.

Brian recruits trainers who deliver safe, effective, and fun workouts every visit. The team brings credentials from NSCA, ACE, NCSF, Yoga Alliance — and experience from Equinox, UCR, UCLA, WIU, and 24 Hour Fitness.

T
Owner · 35 years · ACE-CPT, ACE-CES

Brian Guzman.

Brian is certified by the American Council on Exercise as a Personal Trainer and Corrective Exercise Specialist. His career has spanned 35 years — General Manager, Fitness Manager, Master Trainer, Group Exercise Instructor. He has developed exercise programming for large gym chains and private facilities, and mentored hundreds of trainers. As the owner of Training & Discipline, his mission is simple: recruit exceptional trainers and coaches who deliver safe, effective, fun workouts every single visit.

D
CSCS · M.S. Strength & Sport Psychology · 20 years

Patrick Lauderdale.

Patrick is a Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist with the NSCA. Two undergraduate degrees in Exercise Science and Pedagogy from CSUSB, then a Master's in Strength & Conditioning and Sport Psychology from Western Illinois University. Twenty years in the field — including collegiate strength coaching at UCR, UCLA, and WIU across sports from waterpolo to basketball. Patrick believes it isn't just his job to write the program — it's to help you understand the science behind it.

C S
NCSF-CPT · 500-hr E-RYT · Yoga Therapy · Decade-plus

Zac Armstrong.

Zac is a Certified Personal Trainer with the National Council of Strength and Fitness, with a collegiate background in Exercise Science and Sport Nutrition. He stands as a 500-hour E-RYT and YACEP with Yoga Alliance, with an additional 300 hours of advanced Yoga Therapy training. Over a decade in the fitness realm — strength, yoga, programming, and mentoring fellow professionals.

P S
NSCA-CPT · ACE Health & Wellness · 25 years · Equinox alum

Emma Middlebrook.

Emma has close to 25 years in health and fitness. She runs a small group fitness studio in Portland, OR, and has made Palm Springs home for the desert side of her practice. She especially loves supporting members of the LGBTQA+ family. A former top trainer at Equinox Fitness with NSCA-CPT and ACE Health & Wellness certifications, a BS in Psychology and an Associates in Occupational Therapy. Her specialty: comprehensive workout/lifestyle programs that are scientifically based and tailored exclusively for you.
