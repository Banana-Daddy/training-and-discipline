Personal training,
properly run.

We are not a gym. We are a small, disciplined room — four trainers, one disco ball, glass garage doors that open to the San Jacinto mountains, and a bamboo-floored back room. Every session is one trainer with one client. Phones in the cubby. The hour is yours.

We’re proudly inclusive — locals, seasonal residents, beginners, elite movers, and anyone the broader fitness world has been chilly to. We work with your physical therapist, chiropractor, or surgeon when you need us to. There is no membership and no commitment.
