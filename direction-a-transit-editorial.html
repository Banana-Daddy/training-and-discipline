C Section 01 — About
The studio

A boutique space — built deliberately small so every workout gets the attention it deserves.

We developed Training & Discipline for that client who desires an upscale training experience in a boutique space away from the distractions and hustle of large gyms. We are a studio where every workout is safe, challenging, and fun every time.

We are a community of fitness professionals that provide an environment that is for everyone regardless of body type, experience, or fitness goals. No membership. No commitment. Just sessions, by appointment.

A note from the owner

"Exercise 'buddies' are NOT certified personal trainers. There — we said it."

Brian Guzman · ACE-CPT, ACE Corrective Exercise Specialist · 35 years in the industry

P Section 02 — Programs & Services

The line

Five stops. Customized to your wellness needs. Documented at every visit so progress is clear at a glance.

  1. T
    Stop 01 · Strength
    Strength training — beginner to elite

    Programmed by a CSCS and an ACE-CES. Every plan documented so you can see at a glance the progress you've made.

  2. D
    Stop 02 · Body comp
    Weight loss

    A sustainable plan, written for your life — not a 30-day reset. Built around what you actually like doing.

  3. C
    Stop 03 · Mobility
    Stretching & flexibility

    A dedicated session, not a 60-second warm-up. Useful for the desk-bound, the runners, and the recently-90.

  4. S
    Stop 04 · Yoga
    Individualized yoga instruction

    1-on-1 with a 500-hr E-RYT & Yoga Therapy specialist. In our bamboo-floored room with the Buddha screen — not a Zoom box.

  5. P
    Stop 05 · Medical-condition support
    Programs that work with your doctor

    Completed your cardiac rehab, chiropractic care, physical therapy, recent joint replacement with your doctor or therapist? Have a neurological condition that needs support? We can develop exercise programs that may help you manage your condition and improve your well-being.
