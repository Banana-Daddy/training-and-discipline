N° 02 · The empty rack, after hours
Letter from the studio

Personal training, properly.

Every session at this studio is one trainer with one client, programmed to the body in front of us. No classes. No machine circuits. No phone in your hand. We watch posture. We correct form. We adjust the program when something doesn’t feel right. We work alongside your physical therapist or surgeon when you need us to. The hour is yours, and we treat it that way.

We’re proudly inclusive — locals, seasonal residents, beginners, elites, and anyone the broader fitness world has ever been chilly to. There is no membership and no commitment. You pay one session at a time, or in a 10/20-pack at a small discount. Your first visit is on us — a thirty-minute conversation, a movement screen, and a pick of the line that suits where you actually are.

— Brian Guzman, owner
